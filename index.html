Make your Medication Routine easier

Med Routine will help you stick with your medication routine. Set the therapy and receive notifications to take your meds on time. With Med Routine App your health comes first.

Get the Free App

Features

01

Personalized reminder for your Therapy

MedRoutine allows users to set personalized reminder for their medicines. Decide when you want to receive your notifications. Choose between a louder reminder or a discreet vibration. You won’t miss your meds anymore!

02

Just a thick to mark a medicine as “Taken”

Do you often don’t remember if you have taken or not your medicines? We got your back. MedRoutine implemented an easy and quick way to mark your medicines as “Taken”. You won’t risk to take your medicines twice or skip them altogether anymore.

03

Set reminders to help your loved ones

Do you take care of people following a medicine therapy? With MedRoutine you can set and “Assign” a therapy for another person, in this way it will be easier help them. We’re here to support you, your family and your loved ones.
